## 🎯 Recommended Image Specs (jika ganti ke JPG/PNG):

- **Format**: JPG atau PNG
- **Dimensions**: 1200x800px (3:2 ratio)
- **File Size**: < 500KB per image
- **Quality**: 80-85% (balance antara quality & size)
- **Optimization**: Use TinyPNG.com or ImageOptim
